WELCOME TO XMart Host

Your domain is linked with your XMart Host cPanel, Follow the given guide line to develop, upload or install(wordpress, etc) your website files.

How to access cPanel ?

You can use the following URL to access cPanel:
http://yourdomain.com/cpanel
Find user & password in your email.
After login Goto -> File manager -> public_html and upload your files.
Note: Delete all files from public_html folder before uploading your files.
